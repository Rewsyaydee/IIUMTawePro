## Applied In App

- Primary turquoise: `#00918e`
- Gold accent: `#d59f0f`
- Black and white remain supporting corporate colors.
- Main brand wordmark text uses a Rockwell-first font stack when available.
- Dashboard wording uses the official motto: `Garden of Knowledge and Virtue`.
- Tagline language reflected in the app: `Tawhidic Epistemology`, `Ummatic Excellence`, and `Leading the Way`.
- App header and startup loading screen now use the prepared IIUM logo asset.

## Important Brand Rules

- Do not stretch, distort, shadow, or recolor the official IIUM logo.
- Keep logo/tagline areas clear and uncluttered.
- Use readable fonts and avoid more than 3-5 main color combinations in official visuals.
- Use the prepared transparent logo without stretching, filters, shadows, or color changes that would alter the mark.
